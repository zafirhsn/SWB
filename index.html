A B O U T

It's time for the cannabis sector to get high on the blockchain, and that's what Solana Weed Business is pioneering! Solana Weed Business is integrating the centralized world of dispensaries with the decentralized, by partnering with cannabis dispensaries and creating our very own marketplace where users can purchase cannabis and related products with Solana. This will be the first project that will innovate the cannabis sector by creating a cryptocurrency e-commerce segment for global dispensaries! Think Amazon, but for cannabis and CBD products, where you can buy cannabis online and it's delivered to your door. Dispensaries gain exposure to cryptocurrency on their balance sheet while saving on taxes, and our holders get to reap many benefits through joining our project. NFT holders will have exclusive access to our marketplace and products, 10% of our mint sales will go to our users in the form of a community bank and managed as DAO by our holders, and holders will be able to receive a percentage of revenues that occur on our marketplace! Read more about our project, mission, and team in our SWB Rolling Paper.

F A Q

There are 4,200 uniquely Solana Weed Businesses available to be minted, and we will release an attribute chart with all of our different attributes and rarity! As of now our mint is scheduled for 4/20 and the price will be fixed at 1.5 Solana for each NFT. If you are new to the NFT space and don't know how to get started, hop in our Discord and we'll be happy to guide you through the process.
Each holder will automatically be added to our DAO, where they will manage 10% of our mint sales in the form of a community bank. The opportunities to invest those funds, whether it be buying land in the Metaverse or investing it into alt coins, is all up to the holders. Along with the access to our DAO, holders will have exclusive access to our cannabis marketplace. Lastly, holders will be rewarded with a percentage of revenues generated from running a subscription model on our marketplace site. Read our Litepaper, The Rolling Paper, for more insights on our utilities.
Think of the marketplace as an online e-commerce site where dispensaries can sell their cannabis, and users can buy the cannabis by connecting their wallet and paying with Solana. Each holder will receive a unique token that will grant them access to the marketplace, and from there they can create a profile which will allow them to purchase cannabis online.
We are partnering with some of the top national dispensaries and shops that have premium quality cannabis and products. SWB will run giveaways, offer exclusive products, and even come out with our own Token which will start rewarding people for purchasing through our site! Both the consumer and dispensary do not have to deal with paying taxes, and this is a secure and encrypted transaction. Lastly, many dispensaries still have yet to transition to e-commerce, which is why we are bridging that gap.
